Welcome to the WW1 Bradford Research Group

Preserving the History of World War I

Our mission is to uncover, document, and preserve the experiences of Bradford’s citizens during World War I. Our aim is to honor their service, share their stories, and provide valuable historical insights to the public. Whether you are a historian, student, or enthusiast, we welcome you to explore and contribute to our growing archive of wartime records.
